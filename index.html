Enterprise Retail Demand Forecasting System

Role: Data Scientist | Organization: Accenture | Duration: Feb 2025 – Dec 2025


Overview: Designed large-scale forecasting system generating weekly rolling 57-week forecasts across multiple regions and sales channels.


Technical Highlights:

  • Data ingestion from Snowflake
  • Intermittent demand detection
  • Univariate models (ETS, Holt-Winters, Theta, SARIMA, ARIMA, Prophet)
  • Multivariate models (RF, XGBoost, LightGBM)
  • Weighted ensemble & calibration
  • Hierarchical disaggregation to Sold-To level

Impact: Enabled enterprise planning and reduced volatility using calibration mechanisms.
